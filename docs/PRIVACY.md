# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-05-03

This privacy policy applies to the **Reusable Checklists** mobile application ("the app") published by Anthony Macchia ("the developer").

## Summary

Reusable Checklists is an offline-first app. All checklists and settings you create are stored locally on your device. The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** The app does not request, collect, or transmit any personal information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or telemetry. There are no user accounts and no servers operated by the developer.

## What is stored on your device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

- Checklists you create, including their names, items, and item check states
- App preferences (such as theme selection)

This data never leaves your device through any code path in the app.

## Permissions

The app does not request runtime permissions beyond the defaults granted to all Android applications.

## Backups

The app participates in Android's standard application data backup system (Auto Backup for Apps). If you have enabled Google Drive backup on your device, your operating system may include this app's data in your encrypted device backup. This backup is operated by Google, not by the developer of this app, and is governed by [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). You can disable backup for this app at any time in your device's system settings.

## Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children or users of any age. It is suitable for all ages.

## Data deletion

Because no data leaves your device, you can delete all data the app holds by uninstalling the app or by clearing the app's data through your device's system settings.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a future release, the effective date above will be updated. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes on the Google Play Store.
